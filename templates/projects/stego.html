Project Overview

Imagine you have a secret message and you want to hide it so that no one else can see it, except the person you want to share it with. That's what my project, "Image Steganography using Deep Learning," is all about—like a digital game of hide and seek with messages!

What I did was create a special computer program that can take a message and tuck it away inside a picture without changing how the picture looks. It's like writing a note, folding it super small, and hiding it inside a drawing—except that I used computers to do it!

This way, the message stays hidden and safe from people who might be snooping around for information. Even if someone gets the picture, they won't know there's a secret hidden inside unless they know exactly how to look for it.

Just like magicians have special tricks, I used some computer magic called "deep learning" to make this possible. It's a smart way to help computers think and learn, so they can find the best places in the picture to hide the message.

In the end, the picture with the hidden message can travel across the internet, and only the person who knows the secret can find the message again. It's a fun and smart way to keep our messages private, like our own secret club!
