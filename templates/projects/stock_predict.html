Sentiment Analysis of Stock Market Trends

Leveraging the symbiotic relationship between market sentiment and stock performance, our project delves into analyzing how public opinion on social media can forecast stock market trends. Utilizing a sophisticated blend of technologies, including PySpark for data processing and machine learning models for prediction, we have crafted a data-driven approach to quantify and exploit the insights from Twitter chatter about industry giants like Google, Amazon, Apple, Tesla, and Microsoft.

Github Link

Project Overview

Imagine a world where the collective whispers and shouts on social media give us a glimpse into the future of companies like Google, Amazon, Apple, Tesla, and Microsoft. That's the vision we embarked on, delving into the ocean of tweets to capture the public's sentiments and see how they danced with the stock market's pulse.

In this digital age, every tweet, like, and share is a stroke on the canvas of public opinion. Our quest was to transform these digital brushstrokes into a forecast of market trends. We gathered millions of these social signals, each a piece of the puzzle, and brought them together to see the bigger picture.

With the power of cloud computing, we sifted through these vast datasets as one would pan for gold. But instead of gold, our treasure was insights — the emotional undercurrents that sway stock prices. We were like financial detectives, picking up clues, piecing together a narrative of numbers and emotions.

Our toolkit was diverse — from the simplicity of Logistic Regression, akin to looking through a magnifying glass, to the complexity of Neural Networks, which is more like harnessing a crystal ball. We searched for patterns, for signals amidst the noise. And when our Feed Forward Neural Network emerged with an F1 score of 0.6, we knew we had found something special — a window into the market's soul.

This is not just a tale of data and algorithms. It's about harnessing the collective heartbeat of society to peer into the future of finance. It's about the synergy of human emotion and machine intelligence. And it's about our team, who embarked on this journey together, combining our skills to write a story that begins with a tweet and ends with a prediction.

Join us on this journey. Witness the confluence of technology and human sentiment. See how the whispers of today can become the fortunes of tomorrow.
